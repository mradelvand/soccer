## Warm-Up Phase I: Low-Intensity Movement & Activation

### 1. Forward Jog ‚Üí Backward Jog
- **Repetitions:** 2  
- **Focus:** Basic locomotion and balance  

**Coach's Instruction:**  
"Okay team, let's start with a simple jog! Jog forward from **Cone 1 to Cone 4**. Once you reach Cone 4, turn around and **jog backward** back to Cone 1. Keep it smooth and light! We'll do that **twice**."

---

### 2. Shuffle Right ‚Üí Shuffle Left
- **Repetitions:** 2  
- **Focus:** Lateral movement and footwork  

**Coach's Instruction:**  
"Now let's move side to side. Start **facing the sideline**. Shuffle to Cone 4, leading with your right shoulder. When you reach Cone 4, turn around ‚Äî stay facing the sideline ‚Äî and **shuffle back to Cone 1**, this time leading with your left shoulder. Keep those feet moving fast and stay low!"

## Warm-Up Phase II: Mobility and Dynamic Stretching

### 7. Open Gate ‚Üí Closed Gate
- **Repetitions:** 1  
- **Focus:** Hip mobility and rotation (internal & external)  

**Coach's Instruction:**  
"Time to open and close the 'gate'‚Äîyour hips. **Open Gates to Cone 4**: bring your knee up, rotate it outward, and take three steps between each rep. Switch legs each time. At Cone 4, **Closed Gates back**: bring the knee up from the outside and swing it forward. Keep it controlled!"

### 8. Forward Lunge ‚Üí Lateral Lunge
- **Repetitions:** 1  
- **Focus:** Lower body strength and stability  

**Coach's Instruction:**

### 9. Broad Jump ‚Üí Skater Hops
- **Repetitions:** 1  
- **Focus:** Explosive power and single-leg landing stability  

**Coach's Instruction:**

## Warm-Up Phase III: High-Intensity & Deceleration

### 10. Forward-Backward to Sprint
- **Repetitions:** 1  
- **Focus:** Change of direction and acceleration  

**Coach's Instruction:**  
"This is all about speed and control! Sprint to **Cone 2**, touch it, and backpedal to Cone 1. Do this twice.  
After the second backpedal, **SPRINT all-out to Cone 3**, then slow yourself down and fully stop by **Cone 4**. Jog back to Cone 1."

### 11. Shuffle to Sprint (Right and Left)
- **Repetitions:** 2 (1 right, 1 left)  
- **Focus:** Lateral movement transitioning into linear sprint  

**Coach's Instruction:**  
"Shuffle to **Cone 2** with your right shoulder leading, touch, and shuffle back to Cone 1 with your left shoulder leading. Do this two times.  
Then turn and **sprint to Cone 3**, decelerate, and come to a stop by Cone 4.  
Jog back and repeat the entire sequence starting with the **left shoulder lead**."

- **Repetitions:** 1  
- **Focus:** Max vertical power into horizontal speed  

**Coach's Instruction:**  
"At Cone 1, perform **three powerful Tuck Jumps**‚Äîdrive your knees toward your chest each time.  
On the third landing, explode straight into a **sprint to Cone 3**.  
Decelerate and stop by Cone 4."

### 13. Single Leg Hops to Sprint
- **Repetitions:** 2 (1 right, 1 left)  
- **Focus:** Single-leg power, balance, and sprint transition  

**Coach's Instruction:**  
"Stand on your right leg and perform **three Single Leg Hops**, jumping as high as you can.  
Land softly and immediately burst into a sprint toward **Cone 3**, decelerating by Cone 4.  
Jog back and repeat the sequence on your **left leg**."
